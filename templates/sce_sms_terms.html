Program Overview

Program name: SCE Boise Customer Communications

The SCE Boise SMS program sends recurring SMS text messages to customers and prospective customers of SCE Boise (Supercar Experience Boise), a vehicle rental and experience company based in Boise, Idaho. Messages include booking follow-up, customer service responses, qualification questions, booking confirmations and reminders, re-engagement messages, and promotional offers.

The SMS program is operated on behalf of SCE Boise by Lumen Marketing Solutions.

Opt-In Consent

By providing your phone number to SCE Boise through our online booking form, ad funnel, or any other SCE Boise consent flow, you agree to receive recurring SMS text messages from SCE Boise consistent with this program description.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your activity and engagement with SCE Boise. You may receive a higher frequency of messages immediately after submitting a booking inquiry or abandoning a reservation, and a lower frequency during ordinary engagement.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of the SCE Boise SMS program at any time by replying STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT to any message. After replying with one of these keywords, you will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent unless you re-subscribe.

Help

For help, reply HELP to any message. You may also contact SCE Boise directly using the contact information below.

Re-Subscribe

If you have previously opted out and wish to receive messages again, you may text START to the SCE Boise SMS number, or re-enter your phone number through any SCE Boise booking form to provide fresh consent.

Eligibility

The SCE Boise SMS program is available to users in the United States who are 18 years of age or older. By providing your phone number, you confirm you meet these eligibility requirements.

Privacy

Your information is handled according to the SCE Boise SMS Program Privacy Policy. SCE Boise and Lumen Marketing Solutions do not sell or share your phone number or SMS consent data with any third parties for marketing purposes.
