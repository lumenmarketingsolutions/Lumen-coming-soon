Why $10K doesn't work

  • 15+ integrations. Lofty, Slack, Todoist, Trello, email, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, Google Business, Meta Ads API, Google Ads API, HeyGen, MLS/IDX, Reddit, direct mail vendor. Every one needs auth, error handling, and monitoring. One breaks, everything downstream stops.
  • Lofty's API is limited. Half the list depends on Lofty sync. A lot of what he wants may not even be possible through their API. You won't know until you're deep in it. That alone could burn 20+ hours just figuring out what works and what doesn't.
  • Ad automation is its own project. Meta and Google both require API approval, have rate limits, and compliance hoops. Not a line item.
  • Daily AI content costs real money. Hundreds of API calls a day across platforms. $200 to $500/mo just in token costs, before engineering.
  • Voice-to-content is a product, not a feature. Speech-to-text, intent detection, multi-format output, approval queue. Companies charge $50 to $200/mo for just this.

At $10K, whoever takes this is either building something that demos well and breaks in a week, or they don't know what they're agreeing to. Either way Bryan holds the bag.
