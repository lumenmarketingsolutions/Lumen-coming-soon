Avalon Laser SMS Program

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Avalon Laser and Lumen Marketing Solutions, which operates the SMS program on behalf of Avalon Laser, collect, use, and protect information related to the Avalon Laser SMS messaging program.

Information We Collect

When you provide your phone number to Avalon Laser through our website intake form, ad funnel landing page, or in-clinic consultation form, we collect and store:

  • Your name and phone number
  • The date, time, and source of your opt-in consent
  • Consultation, appointment, and treatment information you provide
  • SMS messages exchanged between you and Avalon Laser
  • Your responses to scheduling, qualification, or follow-up questions

How We Use Your Information

Phone numbers and SMS-related information are used for the following purposes only:

  • Following up on consultation requests submitted by new leads
  • Sending appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Answering customer service questions you initiate
  • Conducting post-treatment check-ins and care follow-ups
  • Sending service-specific offers, treatment promotions, and re-engagement messages to past patients

Data Sharing

Avalon Laser and Lumen Marketing Solutions do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or SMS opt-in consent data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Information may be shared only with service providers required to operate the SMS program (such as Twilio, our SMS infrastructure provider, and our practice management software for appointment scheduling) and only to the extent necessary to deliver messages and care to you.

Data Retention

SMS-related data is retained for as long as your relationship with Avalon Laser is active, plus a reasonable period for record-keeping, customer service history, treatment continuity, and legal compliance. Once retention is no longer required, data is deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Stop receiving SMS messages by replying STOP to any message
  • Request help by replying HELP to any message
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data

Requests can be sent by contacting any of our locations directly.

Children's Privacy

The Avalon Laser SMS program is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect SMS-related information from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent version. Continued use of the SMS program after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
