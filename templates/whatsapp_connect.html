Connect Your WhatsApp

Tap the button below to connect your WhatsApp Business account. This takes about 30 seconds. Your WhatsApp app stays active and nothing changes on your phone.

What happens

1

A Meta login window will open. Sign in if needed.

2

Follow the prompts to connect your WhatsApp Business account.

3

You may receive a verification message on your phone. Confirm it.

4

Done. Your WhatsApp app keeps working exactly as before.
