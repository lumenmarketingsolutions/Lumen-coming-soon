Program Overview

Program name: Avalon Laser Patient Communications

The Avalon Laser SMS program sends recurring SMS text messages to leads, prospective patients, and existing patients of Avalon Laser, a medical aesthetics and laser treatment spa with locations in Encinitas, San Diego, and Carlsbad, California. Messages include consultation booking follow-up, appointment reminders and confirmations, post-treatment check-ins, periodic re-engagement messages for past patients, and notifications about service-specific offers (IPL, lip filler, full-face rejuvenation, undereye PRP, and similar treatments).

The SMS program is operated on behalf of Avalon Laser by Lumen Marketing Solutions.

Opt-In Consent

By providing your phone number to Avalon Laser through our website intake form, ad funnel landing page, in-clinic consultation form, or any other Avalon Laser consent flow, you agree to receive recurring SMS text messages from Avalon Laser consistent with this program description.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your activity and engagement with Avalon Laser. You may receive a higher frequency of messages immediately after submitting a consultation inquiry or scheduling an appointment, and a lower frequency during ordinary engagement.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of the Avalon Laser SMS program at any time by replying STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT to any message. After replying with one of these keywords, you will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent unless you re-subscribe.

Help

For help, reply HELP to any message. You may also contact any Avalon Laser location directly using the contact information below.

Re-Subscribe

If you have previously opted out and wish to receive messages again, you may text START to the Avalon Laser SMS number, or re-enter your phone number through any Avalon Laser intake form to provide fresh consent.

Eligibility

The Avalon Laser SMS program is available to users in the United States who are 18 years of age or older. By providing your phone number, you confirm you meet these eligibility requirements.

Privacy

Your information is handled according to the Avalon Laser SMS Program Privacy Policy. Avalon Laser and Lumen Marketing Solutions do not sell or share your phone number or SMS consent data with any third parties for marketing purposes.
